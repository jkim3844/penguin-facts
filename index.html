Cool Facts About Emperor Penguins

  • Size: Emperor penguins can grow up to 48 inches tall!
  • Weight: They can weigh up to 88 pounds.
  • Swimming Ability: They are excellent swimmers, diving up to 1,800 feet deep.
  • Survival in Cold: Emperor penguins can survive temperatures as low as -60°C (-76°F).
  • Breeding: Males incubate the eggs while females hunt for food.
  • Lifespan: They live up to 20 years in the wild.
  • Diet: Their favorite food includes fish, krill, and squid.
